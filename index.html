What is royalty-based growth capital?

An alternative to equity and traditional debt, royalty-based growth capital provides funding in exchange
for a fixed percentage (a “royalty rate”) of your company’s future monthly revenues. The actual dollar
amount you pay each month adapts to your company’s revenue performance.
